What is Data Science Union

The UCLA Data Science Union is a for-profit, data-driven, student-run organization comprised of the greatest undergraduate minds in math, computer science, statistics, economics, and more. We use big data to make lucrative predictions across competitive industries. We build deep learning networks which buy and sell stocks for us and for our clients.
